FEATURE REFERENCE

Dataset Features

MAGNITUDE
SEISMIC ENERGY RELEASE
Logarithmic measure of energy at the earthquake source. Primary predictor — each unit = ~10× amplitude, ~31× energy.
DEPTH
HYPOCENTER DEPTH (km)
Distance from Earth's surface to rupture point. Shallow events (<70km) cause far more surface damage.
CDI
COMMUNITY DECIMAL INTENSITY
Human-reported shaking intensity. Captures real perceived impact — the human dimension of seismic damage.
MMI
MODIFIED MERCALLI INTENSITY
Instrument-calibrated intensity for local geology. Strongest discriminator between alert classes in this dataset.
SIG
COMPOSITE SIGNIFICANCE
USGS composite metric combining magnitude, intensity and reported effects into one impact score.
ALERT
ML TARGET · 4 CLASSES
Green(14) · Yellow(81) · Orange(260) · Red(59). Based on USGS PAGER fatality/economic loss thresholds.
